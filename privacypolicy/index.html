Privacy Policy for Winnipeg Geodetic Benchmark Database

Effective Date: June 10, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Winnipeg Geodetic Benchmark Database app! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information from our users.

3. Use of Google Maps

Our app uses Google Maps to display Surveying Geodetic Benchmarks around the city of Winnipeg. The use of Google Maps is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Please review Google’s Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

4. Advertisements

Our app displays ads from third-party advertisers to support the maintenance of our servers. These ads appear when the app is first loaded and as a banner at the bottom of the screen. The ads are provided by Google AdMob, and we do not collect any personal information from these ads. Please review Google AdMob’s Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not store or secure any user data. The security of the app itself is maintained through standard security measures to ensure it operates as intended.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has used our app, we will take steps to delete any information inadvertently collected.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
